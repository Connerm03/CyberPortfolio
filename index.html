About Me

I'm a Computer Science student at California State University San Marcos (CSUSM), with a focused interest in cybersecurity and secure systems engineering. I believe that understanding systems from the ground up — the algorithms, the architecture, the vulnerabilities — is what separates a great security professional from a good one.

My approach is foundation-first: I invest deeply in understanding how things work before trying to break or protect them. From low-level network packet analysis to high-level threat modeling, I'm passionate about defending digital infrastructure and building things that are secure by design.

Outside of academics, I enjoy weightlifting, fishing, photography, and gaming — activities that train both discipline and creative problem-solving. I'm actively seeking a cybersecurity internship where I can contribute, grow, and make a real impact.
